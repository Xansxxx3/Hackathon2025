How to Play

Player 1 (Fire Element)

  • A/D - Move left/right
  • W - Jump
  • F - Float ability

Player 2 (Water Element)

  • Arrow Keys - Move left/right
  • Up Arrow - Jump
  • G - Duck ability

Game Mechanics

  • Work together to reach both doors
  • Avoid elements that don't match your type
  • Use transformation zones to change elements
  • Press R to reset the level
